ABOUT WEB DESIGN

Web design process involves creating a sites architecture, designing its layout and user interfaces and coding its work correctly.

Project Goal

To provide information/advice/resources to markerspaces and markerspaces
To raise the profile of making in the UK
To understand the current landscape of making in the uk.
